TYPE TO SURVIVE!!

Happy Harry on the left is having a lovely walk in the park, when suddenly, out of nowhere, all sorts of random objects/animals begin to attack! All of these objects/animals are super dangerous to him! It's up to you to save him!!

INSTRUCTIONS: - as soon as an object/animal appears, type a word solution followed by the enter/submit key/button. (Example: if the object is fire, you may want to try typing "water" and press enter!) If the word solution is correct and is typed before the object/animal reaches Harry, it won't hurt him! If you do not type the correct solution before the object/animal reaches him, he will lose a life and his face will change. Harry has 5 lives in total. See how far you can get!
